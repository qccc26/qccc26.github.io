Abstract Submission & Attendance Application

Abstract submission for oral contributions deadline is: March 29, 2026.

To submit your contribution please:

  1. Use the abstract template (see below) for preparing your abstract.
  2. Save your abstract as surname_oral.
  3. Send your abstract to budhaditya.bhattacharjee@uni.lu with the subject "QCCC2026 ORAL contribution application". In the email, clearly specify your name and affiliation and if you need a visa to attend.

For non-contributive participation, please write an email to budhaditya.bhattacharjee@uni.lu, with the subject "QCCC2026 ATTENDANCE application", indicating your name, surname, affiliation, and your wish to participate.

You will be notified of acceptance of your contribution and/or participation before April 1, 2026. Instructions to complete registration will be provided in the acceptance email.
